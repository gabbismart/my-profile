GABRIEL BRIGHT SIANU

Profile

GABRIEL BRIGHT Sianu was born on 27 January 1986 in Lagos, Nigeria, the second of three children to Egun parents, Dorcas and Isaac Dansu Sianu. While the family's ancestral hometown is Ibode-Ase in Ipokia Local Government of Ogun State, Gabriel grew up in No 6, Ikosi Ketu, of Lagos State. Gabriel completed his secondary education at the Community High School Tube, in Ipokia of Ogun State, receiving several academic prizes. He went on to study Secretarial studies at Alaketu Comprehensive Continue Education Center Idiroko of Nigeria for two years. After this period, he was employed as a legitimate secretary to a Lawyer at Isaac Obrutu & Co (legal practitioners).

At the age of nineteen, Gabriel left for a career in information technology, join PCB for two years course on Computer Engineering and Networking Training. He gained admission to study Telecommunication at TCC for another two years, and he went on to pursue a degree in business administration and management at The University of Lagos.



Gabriel graduated with honor in 2016, and then completed an executive degree in leadership development at International Institute of Professional Management Nigeria/USA.

It is during his year after graduating from TCC that he started working with ETNS Projects Solution Nigeria Ltd, in Nov 2011. He as able to transit from an internship to a projects manager, and later move to work with Cambridge Broadband Network Limited Africa in the year 2019.

Gabriel is an Associate fellow at International Institute of Professional Manager since the 2020 academic year, and earned an ELD in Business Management in 2020.

Gabriel is married to Oluwaseun Osundinakin Sianu and has a son Ereoluwa. He divides his time between his family and his job where he often relax coding as a Devop Engineer in Nigeria.
